SWE-RM studies execution-free reward modeling for software engineering agents and shows that reward models useful for test-time scaling do not automatically transfer to reinforcement learning. The paper identifies calibration and classification accuracy as key properties for RL training, then introduces a mixture-of-experts reward model that improves both test-time scaling and RL performance on SWE benchmarks.
