LMFlow is an extensible and lightweight toolkit for fine-tuning and inference of large foundation models. It supports continuous pretraining, instruction tuning, parameter-efficient fine-tuning, alignment tuning, inference acceleration, and multimodal extensions, with carefully designed APIs for customization.
