Qwen3-Coder-Next is an open-weight coding-agent language model with 80B total parameters and 3B active parameters at inference time. The report describes large-scale agentic training with executable environments, mid-training, and reinforcement learning, and shows strong results on coding-agent benchmarks such as SWE-Bench and Terminal-Bench.
