This paper introduces the Misleading Chart Question Answering (Misleading ChartQA) benchmark, a large-scale multimodal dataset designed to assess MLLMs in identifying and reasoning about misleading charts. It contains over 3,000 curated examples, covering 21 types of misleaders and 10 chart types.
