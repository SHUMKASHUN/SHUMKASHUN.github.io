Despite strong task accuracy, fine-tuned large language models often suffer from tuning-induced mis-calibration. We investigate why this happens and propose Efficient Trustworthy Distillation (FIRST), which uses a small portion of the teacher's knowledge to obtain a more reliable language model in a cost-efficient way.
